# 📊 Risk Assessment

## 🔐 Identified Risk

Unauthorized access to CCTV systems due to exposure of stored credentials.

---

## 📈 Risk Analysis

- Likelihood: High  
- Impact: Critical  

➡️ Risk Level: 🔴 CRITICAL

---

## 🧠 Justification

- Credentials are stored in a visible/recoverable format
- Lack of access control mechanisms
- Access to multiple environments (stores, office, factory)
- Vulnerability not yet remediated

---

## 🎯 Business Impact

- Privacy violations
- Exposure of internal operations
- Potential misuse of surveillance systems
- Reputational damage
